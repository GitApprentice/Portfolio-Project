In 2015 I embarked on my first of many solo overseas journeys to Oslo, Norway. It was a life changing experience, not to sound cliche. The only thing on my mind when I got back was how can I get back there? I had 2 problems. First, I didn't have the job qualifications they wanted in order for me to obtain a visa. Secondly, I didn't have money saved up so that I could live there for an extended period of time without working. The only thing on my mind was how can I make money and travel at the same time?
